A little about me!

Hello world! My name is Zuri Alexander. I'm 16 and i'm going to be a junior next year. I was born in Virginia (but don't tell anyone) and I grew up in Palo Alto, California and Washington DC. I was homeschooled until the 4th grade. This was the start of my tragic school experience. I went to family friendly quaker school until middle school rolled around and my mom and i were too tired off the long comute. For four years I went to a private school in DC. Eventually I gave on up on school in the US and decided to pack my bag and head to a french boarding school in a small town in Nromandy. It is there I have stayed until COVID 19. Enough about school... When I am not singlehandedly performing every role possible in musicals for my mom in our living room, I'm am probably out in the woods or on a lake, playing volleyball, reading a book, writing confusing poetry, or enjoying the company of my friends and family. I used play piano and do ballet, but I quit in 8th grade and have immensly regretted it since. My secret passion/guilty pleasure is visual arts but I I'm really not that great at it... I love to paint and sculp and make pots and sing, but I also suck. The only thing preventing people from completly scoweling at my masterpieces is the fact they can see that I only do it for myself.
