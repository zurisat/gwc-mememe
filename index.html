A little about me!

Hello, world! My name is Zuri Alexander. I'm 16, and I'm going to be a junior next year. I was born in Virginia (but don't tell anyone), and I grew up in Palo Alto, California, and Washington, DC. I was homeschooled until the 4th grade. For six years, I bounced around schools in Maryland and DC. Eventually, I gave on up on school in the US and decided to pack my bag and head to a French boarding school in a small town in Normandy. It is there I have stayed until COVID 19. Enough about school... When I am not singlehandedly performing every role possible in musicals for my mom in our living room, I'm am probably out in the woods or on a lake, playing volleyball, reading a book, writing confusing poetry, or enjoying the company of my friends and family. I used to play piano and do ballet, but I quit in 8th grade and have immensely regretted it since. My secret passion/guilty pleasure is visual arts, but I'm really not that great at it... I love to paint and sculpt and make pots and sing (all things which are not my specialty). The only thing preventing people from completely scowling at my masterpieces is seeing that I only do it for myself. I try to live my life in experiences. Some so many people just live to tell stories and impress. I, on the other hand, value those at the moment act. I'm not saying that that's not problematic, but it is just how I flow... Love of love, y'all. Toodles :)
